Sustainably Sourced
Indonesia's Best Coconut, Produce, & Seafood

Biora Prima Mandiri Corp. is a company specializing in marine and agricultural products, dedicated to delivering high-quality goods from Indonesia to national and global markets. Through partnerships with local fishermen and farmers, we ensure responsibly sourced products that uphold sustainability and empowerment. With a focus on fair, eco-friendly, and quality-driven trade, we bridge producers and consumers while driving local economic growth and showcasing Indonesia's natural wealth internationally. Backed by a professional team and a strong distribution network, we are committed to providing fresh, healthy, and premium products.
